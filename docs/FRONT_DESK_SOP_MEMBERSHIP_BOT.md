# Silver Mirror Front Desk SOP
## Membership Bot Follow-Up Calls

Document owner: Operations  
Version date: 2026-03-06

---

## Purpose

Provide clear, consistent guidance for front desk and operations staff when guests call after interacting with the membership chatbot.

---

## Core SLA Language

- "We'll follow up within 24-48 hours."
- "For fastest support, call (888) 677-0055."

---

## Scenario 1: Inactive account + outstanding balance

### Process
1. Confirm account status and balance.
2. Offer two paths:
   - Reactivate after balance settlement (continue membership benefits).
   - Finalize cancellation after balance settlement.
3. If guest chooses cancellation, process that path directly.
4. Do not require a cancellation reason to proceed.

### Suggested script
"Your account is currently inactive with an outstanding balance. Once that's settled, we can either reactivate your membership so you keep benefits, or finalize cancellation. Which path would you like?"

---

## Scenario 2: Reaction/safety complaint

### Process
1. Prioritize manager escalation immediately.
2. Capture callback number and best time.
3. Do not use retention commitment language (no 3-cycle language).
4. Confirm manager follow-up within 24 hours when possible.

---

## Scenario 3: Credits while paused

### Policy
- Credits can be used during an approved pause if still within 90 days from the initial bill date.

### Suggested script
"Yes, you can use credits during an approved pause as long as they're still within 90 days of the original bill date."

---

## Scenario 4: Booking/payment technical issue

### Process
1. Collect:
   - location
   - device/browser
   - screenshot
   - exact error wording
2. Log as website bug incident.
3. Give fastest path: phone support.
4. Confirm 48-hour follow-up target.

---

## Scenario 5: Non-membership unresolved issue

### Process
1. Escalate to general support.
2. Route to `hello@silvermirror.com` (not memberships@ unless membership-account-specific).

---

## Scenario 6: Active membership cancellation

### Process
1. Confirm decision captured.
2. Confirm handoff to memberships team for backend processing.
3. Confirm 48-hour follow-up email.
4. Remind:
   - processing takes 30 days
   - unused credits remain valid for 90 days after cancellation

---

## Routing

- Membership account/cancellation: `memberships@silvermirror.com`
- General/time-sensitive non-membership: `hello@silvermirror.com`
- Urgent: `(888) 677-0055`
